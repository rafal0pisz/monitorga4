# Pricing — AlertGA4

All prices in PLN, excluding VAT. Billed monthly or annually — annual billing is discounted to roughly 10 months' worth of the monthly price. No long-term commitment; cancel anytime. A 14-day free trial (Agency-tier limits, no card required) is available once per account.

## Individual
- Price: 49 PLN/month (billed monthly) | 490 PLN/year (≈41 PLN/month, billed annually)
- Limits: up to 3 GA4 properties; up to 5 custom events/parameters/e-commerce checks per property
- Features: daily automated monitoring, email alerts on detected issues, configuration wizard with suggestions pulled from the connected GA4 property, email support

## Pro
- Price: 139 PLN/month (billed monthly) | 1390 PLN/year (≈116 PLN/month, billed annually)
- Limits: up to 10 GA4 properties; unlimited custom events/parameters/e-commerce checks per property
- Features: same as Individual, with no cap on custom checks per property, email support

## Agency
- Price: 399 PLN/month (billed monthly) | 3990 PLN/year (≈333 PLN/month, billed annually)
- Limits: up to 100 GA4 properties; unlimited custom events/parameters/e-commerce checks per property
- Features: same as Pro, plus a dedicated support channel and a 1-hour/month consultation with a GA4 analyst

## Included on every plan
- Read-only Google sign-in (`analytics.readonly` scope) — AlertGA4 never modifies a connected GA4 property, its configuration, or its underlying data
- Daily automated checks: missing/expected events, duplicate purchase events, self-referral traffic, direct-traffic anomalies, night-time bot traffic, bounce-rate anomalies, conversion-rate drops, e-commerce funnel continuity (view_item → add_to_cart → view_cart → purchase), event-parameter coverage
- Email alert when a project's quality score drops below its configured threshold
